Before starting to play, lets take a look on how each piece moves and the board.

There are 6 distinct pieces in chess.

  1. First we have the pawn there are total 8 pawns for each player so total of 16 pawn on the board
  2. The rook - Its an important piece which has 5 points and there 2 rooks for each player.
  3. The bishop - Its also a powerful piece and has 3 points, each player has 2 bishops on dark squared and light sqauared
  4. The kight (horsey) - This piece comes with an advantage to jump over other pieces we will see how below there are 2 horses for each player each has 3 points
  5. The Queen - the most powerful piece with 9 points each player has one queen at the start
  6. The King - The piece on which the whole game is actually played
